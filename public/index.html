From the journal

Design tips you can use right away

Smart, practical updates on layouts, lighting, and finishes. Browse quick reads that help you make confident decisions, avoid costly mistakes, and create rooms that feel calm, useful, and beautifully personal.

Layer Light Like a Pro

Use ambient, task, and accent lighting together to build warmth, improve function, and highlight your room’s best architectural details.

Accent Textiles That Work

Mix texture, scale, and tones instead of matching everything. Pillows and throws should complement your room, not compete for attention.

Small Bathroom, Big Style

Choose a statement mirror, reduce visual clutter, and add one organic element to make compact bathrooms feel brighter and more intentional.

Questions? We Have Answers.

How does your design process work?

We start with a discovery call, then a concept proposal, design development, and finally installation. You’re involved every step.

Do you offer virtual consultations?

Absolutely. We work with clients locally and remotely via video calls and digital mood boards.

What is the typical investment?

Projects vary widely; we provide a detailed proposal after our first meeting. Our fees are always transparent.

How long does a project take?

A single room can take 4-8 weeks; full homes 4-6 months depending on scope and custom pieces.
